About us!

What we do

AD club provide the platform to the young talent who have interest in arts, painting, Drama and writing.We take the problem of society to the people through street play(Nukkad). Through the Nukkad Natak, we make people aware of those social issues, due to which our country is left behind today. We encourage all types of art forms and painting, so that every artist can perform their style of art in their best way. As an elite club, it has always been our endeavor to bring the bad elements of the society to the people so that that society can grow and to give all the artists a great upset so that they can showcase their art in the best way. As we know, There are many ways to reach the destination, our club would also execute its floor through digital medium. This is our new destination by presenting the social issue to the public on a digital platform by creating short videos and delivering any message on it in such a way that it remains attractive and people understand it easily.And people should use it in their lives so that our society is uplifted.

Our Goal

Dedicated peoples working as a team to deliver social messege through the sense of art . And to entertain people that create values for betterments .
